A Slower Kind of Time

Analog Revival Expo invites you into a different pace of living-one shaped by texture, light, and presence. Inspired by the warmth of film, the softness of paper, and the quiet glow of analog devices, the event create a space where time feels slower and more intentional. From vinly records to film cameras, the expo brings together enthusiasts, creators, and curious minds to reconnect with the charm of pre-digital tools. Through curated exhibits and hands-on experiences, visitors are encouraged to pause, explore, and fully immerse themselves in a tactile world that exists beyond the screen.
